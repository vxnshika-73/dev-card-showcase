Open Source Beginner Friendly Community First

Learn by contributing,
grow with the community.

Community Card Showcase is a small open-source project where developers add their profile cards and learn how to make Pull Requests in a safe, friendly environment.

Goal

Help beginners start their open-source journey without fear.

You’ll practice

Git, GitHub, Pull Requests, collaboration, and clean code basics.

Skill Level

Perfect for first-time contributors and students.

Your journey here

01

Discover the project

Find the Community Hall of Fame and see how others designed their first cards and contributions.

02

Fork & create your card

Fork the repository, add your own profile card using simple HTML/CSS, and personalize your message.

03

Open your first PR

Push your changes and open a Pull Request. Maintainers will review, guide, and merge your work.

04

Grow with the community

Stay connected, explore more issues, and continue contributing to open-source projects with confidence.

Start your open-source story today

You don’t need to be an expert. One small, sincere contribution is enough to begin your journey and become part of the community.
